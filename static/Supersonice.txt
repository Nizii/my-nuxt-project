Supersonic is a racing game where two players duel against each other. 
On one hand, one player controls the spaceship, and on the other, there's the track builder, 
who tries to destroy the spaceship by creating curves. 
The development team consisted of 3 computer scientists and a designer. 
I was in charge of the physics. The game was developed using Unity and C#.
Development period: 6 weeks.
